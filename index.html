Childhood

On June 19th, 1988, A. N. Daniel was born to Officer David Daniel of the US Army, and wife Beverly Daniel, in Luxembourg, Germany. She returned home to family in Acworth, Georgia shortly thereafter. Ashley I am told was a quiet child, who followed her routine with ease and did not want for much more than to have her questions answered.




EDUCATION

During most of her youth she attended one elementary, middle, and high school and was a favorite among the youth beauty pageant circuit. She grew up with one younger sibling and cared for various stray animals along the way. Ashley was a girl scout where she developed a love for camping, a green belt in karate, a softball catcher, a basketball point guard, a volleyball setter, and a sports medicine trainer. Later she attended dental careers institute, and obtained a Associate of Science from Georgia Highlands College.




WORK

Ashley, has lived all of her life in the Atlanta region, and has made a respectable living as a office manager in the dental field since 2015. Helping others is a passion of mine and seeing them leave with a healthy smile is rewarding.



PRESENT

She still resides in the Atlanta region and is blessed to have a beautiful and healthy baby boy. In her spare time she enjoys baking cookies, listening to country music, spending time with family and friends, and her miniature schnauzers.
