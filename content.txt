On June 19th, 1988, A. N. Daniel was born to Officer David Daniel of the US Army, and wife Beverly Daniel, in Luxembourg, Germany.  
She returned home to family in Acworth, Georgia shortly thereafter.  Ashley I am told was a quiet child, who followed her routine with ease and did not want for much more than to have her questions answered.
During most of here youth she attended various elementary schools and was a favorite among the youth beauty pagent circuit. Ashley, has lived all of her life in the Atlanta region, 
and has made a respectable living as a dental office manager.
